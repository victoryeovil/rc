02/28/2020
Pastor Erik B. Chikwira

Loving Jesus with all your heart, mind, and soul

The sermon centers on the commandment to love Jesus with all our heart, mind, and soul. This directive serves as the foundation of our faith and requires a holistic approach to our relationship with Christ. Loving Him with our heart involves emotional devotion, while loving with our mind demands intellectual engagement through studying His Word. Lastly, loving with our soul signifies a total surrender of our identity to His lordship. This comprehensive love has the power to transform our lives, influencing our decisions and character. Challenges may arise, but remembering His sacrificial love keeps us devoted. Ultimately, loving Jesus completely leads to a purpose-filled, joyous journey of faith..

Living for Christ requires boldness

The sermon discusses the essential aspect of "Living for Christ Requires Boldness." It highlights how, as believers, we are called to courageously stand up for our faith, even in the face of challenges. Understanding the Bible requires dedication

The message underscores the significance of dedication in "Understanding the Bible." It explores how the Bible, while profound, requires a committed and patient approach to grasp its depth and meaning. The sermon emphasizes the role of prayer, study, and seeking wisdom from God in comprehending the Scriptures. Attendees are encouraged to allocate time for consistent study, engage in reflection, and seek guidance from fellow believers. By devoting themselves to understanding the Word, they can unlock its transformative power and apply its principles to their lives.
